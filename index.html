About myself

I believe that one should dedicate one’s life to a subject that makes them forget about time while practicing it. The process of solving math problems and designing algorithms completely absorbs me. I spend my free time trying to solve logic puzzles and learning about new things that I do not have the chance to study at university or when I am too impatient to wait for a course.

The summer I decided to study computer science, I had been learning Java on my own. In my sophomore year, I enrolled in the freeCodeCamp program to study web development. Soon, I developed an interest in machine learning. I started taking online courses to learn more about subjects like reinforcement learning, deep neural networks and natural language understanding and processing. Because I started working on small projects early on, I managed to gain experience and employment in software development while studying in parallel. This job has given me the opportunity to work on exciting projects and also to conduct a lot of research. I had the chance to work with artificial neural networks and the RASA stack for building chat bots.

Working part-time and maintaining a good academic record has disciplined me and made me become even more organized and efficient. At the moment, I am learning more about machine learning and data science. I hope that one day I will contribute to building a machine learning platform or service myself.
